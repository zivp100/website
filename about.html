My Story

With over two decades in software development, machine learning, and healthcare technology, I've seen firsthand how AI transforms the way teams build, ship, and collaborate.

As Chief Data Scientist at a healthcare startup, I led the development of HIPAA-compliant AI systems for eye care diagnostics — work that sat at the intersection of rigorous engineering and real patient impact.

Today, I channel that experience into helping engineering leaders and their teams navigate the AI revolution. My talks aren't theoretical — they're built from years of hands-on experience cutting iteration cycles in half, reducing defects by 40%, and fundamentally rethinking how we hire and develop engineering talent.
