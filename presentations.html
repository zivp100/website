Keynote

Hiring Engineers When AI Changes Everything

When most of the software is written by AI tools, how do you find good candidates? Asking coding questions is no longer a good metric to evaluate candidates and skill. This talk presents a redesigned approach to technical hiring that evaluates what matters in an AI-augmented world.

Your Audience Will Learn:

✓ Why traditional coding interviews fail to identify top talent in an AI-first world
✓ New assessment frameworks that evaluate problem-solving, adaptability, and AI tool proficiency
✓ How to measure learning velocity — the skill that matters most in 2026
✓ Practical hiring rubrics you can implement in your next recruiting cycle
⏱ 45–60 min 🎓 CTOs, VPs of Eng, HR Leaders 🎤 Keynote or Panel
Workshop

Faster Delivery with AI

The gap between what product envisions and what engineering delivers costs companies months. This talk shows how AI creates a shared language that closes that gap dramatically.

Your Audience Will Learn:

✓ AI-generated acceptance tests that align product requirements with engineering output
✓ Code clarity tools that make PRs readable by non-engineers
✓ How one team went from 2-week feedback cycles to 3-day deployments
✓ Building a "shared AI workspace" between product and engineering
⏱ 60–90 min 🎓 Product & Engineering Teams 🎤 Workshop or Breakout
