01

AI-Augmented Development Pipeline

The Challenge

A mid-size SaaS company was struggling with slow iteration cycles. Code reviews took days, documentation was always stale, and engineers were spending more time on boilerplate than on solving real problems. The team had access to AI tools but no coherent strategy for using them.

The Approach

Following the framework from my “Developing Software in the Age of AI” talk, the team integrated LLMs into three key areas: automated code scaffolding for new features, AI-assisted code review as a first pass before human reviewers, and auto-generated documentation tied to every PR merge.

Results After 90 Days

50% Reduction in Development Time
40% Reduction in Code Defects
3x More Up-to-Date Docs
02

Redesigning Technical Hiring for an AI World

The Challenge

A Series B startup was losing top candidates to slow hiring loops and outdated coding assessments. Their traditional whiteboard interviews couldn’t differentiate between engineers who could think critically and those who simply memorized patterns — a gap widened further by AI coding assistants.

The Approach

Using the hiring framework from my “Hiring Engineers When AI Changes Everything” talk, the team redesigned their pipeline: adaptive coding assessments that work alongside Copilot, learning-velocity metrics replacing rote problem-solving, and AI-powered scoring for both technical and behavioral signals.

Results After 60 Days

50% Faster Time-to-Hire
35% Higher Offer Acceptance
2x Better 90-Day Retention
03

Closing the Product × Engineering Gap

The Challenge

A healthcare tech company was stuck in 2-week feedback loops between product and engineering. Product requirements were ambiguous, PRs were opaque to non-engineers, and misalignment was costing the team months of rework every quarter.

The Approach

After the “Faster Dev × Product Iteration” workshop, the team adopted three changes: AI-generated acceptance tests that translated product requirements into verifiable specs, code clarity tools that made PRs readable by PMs, and a shared AI workspace for async alignment between sprints.

Results After 90 Days

80% Shorter Feedback Cycles
25% Increase in Team Velocity
60% Less Quarterly Rework
