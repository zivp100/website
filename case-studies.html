Building Better Software Teams

The Challenge

A mid-size engineering org struggled with knowledge silos. Senior developers hoarded context, onboarding took months, and team velocity was stagnant despite hiring.

The Approach

Deployed AI-powered skill gap analysis to identify hidden bottlenecks. Built automated documentation pipelines that captured institutional knowledge in real-time. Introduced collaborative AI workflows that made expertise shareable.

The Results

35% Faster onboarding
25% Higher team velocity
3x Knowledge sharing frequency
02

Hiring in the Age of AI Tools

The Challenge

Traditional coding interviews couldn't distinguish between developers who understood AI tools and those who just copied from them. The hiring pipeline was slow, expensive, and producing poor signal.

The Approach

Redesigned the entire evaluation process with AI-native assessments. Introduced adaptive challenges where candidates work with AI tools (not against them). Built continuous learning metrics that predict long-term performance.

The Results

40% Shorter hiring cycle
2x Higher 1-year retention
60% Better hiring manager satisfaction
03

Faster Dev × Product Iteration

The Challenge

Product and engineering spoke different languages. Requirements were vague, feedback came too late, and the average feature took weeks of back-and-forth before reaching production.

The Approach

Introduced AI-generated acceptance tests that translated product intent into testable specifications. Deployed code clarity tools that made pull requests readable by non-engineers. Created a shared AI workspace for real-time alignment.

The Results

60% Shorter feedback cycles
3 days Avg. time to deployment
80% Fewer requirement misunderstandings
